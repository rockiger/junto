Welcome to your wiki

This is yourwiki, where you can do whatever you want. All content you create here is saved on your Google Drive.

You can use it to

  • Develop ideas
  • Use it as a notebook
  • Collect links, files and data
  • Post code snippets you find useful

These are just suggestions. Do whatever you want. It is your wiki.

Some tips to get you started

  • Edit this page. Click on the edit icon or press e on your keyboard, the editor will open. You can insert files from your Google Drive by using the Drive-button in the menu bar.
  • Insert a new page. Click on the New Page button on the top left to create a new page below the current page. If you want to create a page somewhere else in your wiki, hover over an entry in the page tree and click the +-button.
  • Search your wiki. Click into the search box or press / on your keyboard to search your wiki. The search box will also show your last visited wiki pages.
  • You can insert files from your Google Drive into your wiki pages by using the Drive-button in the menu bar.
  • If you need more wikis, add them from your Google Drive using the New-Button.

Markdown shortcuts

If you know markdown you can use it as a shortcut to structure your content. You simply type the markdown marker and hit space. Fulcrum supports the following markers:

# Header 1

## Header 2

### Header 3

#### Header 4
